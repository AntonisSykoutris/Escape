All for the thrill of the escape

A new kind of entertainment for all ages

Escape rooms are a new form of entertainment for people of all ages. Players are tasked to find clues, experience a story, solve puzzles and have fun.It's the perfect choice for fun and to escape reality!

Our rooms combine logic and teamwork

Teams can choose whether they want to test their mental abilities, or laugh or experience their worst nightmares from a wide selection of scenarios.

Immersive experience

When an individual enters an escape room, they should feel transported to the central plot of the room.

Work together

The point of any escape room is to work together with your friends, solve the clues, and get out!

Clever puzzles

A good escape room rely on clue finding and good old-fashioned mental puzzles to challenge all players.
